This page will provide you with a full scope of Israel's top artists and concert venues. Tel Aviv, Israel is the center of events, music, and star artists. This site will include information on Tel Aviv's concert life, popular venues, and the Top Artists. After reading this page, you will be ready to explore Tel Aviv's entertainment scene!

Concert Life

The last time I was in Israel, my cousins took my family to a random concert. Instead of steel bleachers or stadium seating, it was outside with rock formations as levels to sit on. The artists we wound up seeing was Aviv Geffen. At the time, we had no idea who this was, now we realize he’s an Israeli mega-star. What is great about concerts is Israel is the closeness and intimacy of the events. When in Israel, pick a random concert and event. You may stumble upon the country’s leading star!

Popular Venues

Tel Aviv has a unique interior design style. The new concert buildings have older style to them. Tel Aviv's Mann Auditorium is pictured above and features a cycle of popular artists every night. Other popular venues include Reading 3, which has huge light panels hanging from the ceiling. Also, Barby, Live Park Rishon LeZion, and Menora Mivtachim Arena are the major players. My tip to visitors of Israel, especially Tel Aviv, look at the website of these venues and pick a concert to go to!

Top Artists

I am a college student who enjoys a variety of music styles. From upbeat to acoustic, my taste ranges. So, I’m going to provide you some recommendations. Omer Adam is a hot ticket. He is a mega-star for a younger generation of Israelis who like to have fun and party! The most successful of singers in Israel with a slower, traditional vibe is Eyal Golan. He actually just married Daniel Greenberg, a beautiful Israeli model and welcomed two children. To get you started, listen to this Israeli playlist to find your artist/genre.

This page will provide you with a full scope for visiting Tel Aviv. You may have heard this city in songs or movies as is it such a popular vacation spot. This page will give you insight into the Segway city, its fanciest OCD TLV restaurant, and the city's lively nightlife. After reading this page, you will be ready to visit Tel Aviv!

Segway City

Tel Aviv is a diverse city with a variety of things to enjoy. If you like to the outdoors there is Charles Clore Park, the Dizengoff Center for shoppers, and downtown for late night fun! I’d like to share a story about my time in Tel Aviv. On Port Namal, a boardwalk bordering the Mediterranean Sea, they offer Segway tours. My whole family and my extended Israeli family decided to take a Segway for a spin. Long story short, Port Namal has so many hazardous hurdles for a young Segway enthusiast, that I took a massive spill. Since then, I have never operated a Segway.

OCD TLV

Now, for a recovery meal, you may be looking for something to just take the pain away from your Segway fall. Keep in mind that Tel Aviv is home to the finest of restaurants. So if you wanted to treat yourself after a tumble, you can do so like royalty. OCD TLV is the most popular restaurant in Tel Aviv. Groups line up around the restaurant hoping to get a table. The menu is home to a variety of specialty, gourmet dishes. If you want an experience with foaming egg yolks and caramelized asparagus, this experimental kitchen is where you want to be.

The Nightlife

After your special fine dining night out, you may be looking to continue the night. You’re in the right place. Tel Aviv is the capital of nightlife! Tel Aviv captures a young, partying crowd of young adults and students. In Tel Aviv, you will find many clubs, concerts and events. Tel Aviv is where the top stars, domestic and international, play.

My Time in Israel

Israel holds a special place in my heart. I remember getting off of the plane at Ben Gurion Airport feeling like I was home. As we left the plane, we were soon met by Mona. Mona is my fifth cousin who lives in Israel. Despite never meeting beforehand, our family clicked instantly with her. Our time in Israel has many hilarious stories, warm memories, and family time I had never had prior! In the photo you will see my sister and myself during a canoeing excursion. Our cousin Mona decided to book us for the advanced course which was hilariously frightening for us beginners.

My Passions

Not only an I passionate about my time in Israel. I'm passionate about food and Mediterranean cuisine. On each of the pages I have created for you, you will see my insider tip for a place to eat in that area. I have learned through travel experience that without food, your travel companions might get a bit cranky. It is always important to know a visitor-approved spot to eat when hunger strikes. Hummus is such the popular food of Israel. You can get bowls of it fancy, like above, or just plain with a splash of olive oil and onions. How do you like yours?

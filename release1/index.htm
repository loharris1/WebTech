Light Background: Israel

In 1948, former prime minster, David Ben-Gurion proclaimed the establishment of the State of Israel. Israel is geographically located in Asia. The country borders Jordan to the east, Lebanon to the north, Syria to the northeast, and Egypt to the southwest. The primary or official language spoken is Hebrew. Arabic is the semi-official language of the country. Foreign languages that you can find spoken in Israel include both Russian and English.

Some fun facts I know about Israel is that they have the second highest concentration of high-tech companies in the world, first is within Silicon Valley. If you are an environmentalist, you may be pleased to know Israel is the only country in the world with more trees than it had 50 years ago. The country is incredibly fascinating and accomplished.

Do not be fooled by its small area! The country is comparable to the size of my home state, New Jersey. I have visited Israel twice and am excited to be your personal guide through information I know and locations I have found along my journeys.

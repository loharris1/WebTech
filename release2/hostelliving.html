What's Hostel Living?

Hostel living in Israel is popular, but nowhere is it more popular than in the Sea of Galilee region. In this area, you will find a massive amount of university students, individual tourists, and young adults living in hostels. Hostels are lower-priced living solutions that offer basic, shared accommodations. If you are traveling alone and would like an immersive, collective experience with others ‚Äì a hostel is your best bet. Hostels can range in privacy, price, and accommodations available.

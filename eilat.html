Now, I like Eilat a lot. It is a wild city with much to do. On this page, I'll provide what you'd need to know for a day centered around visiting this city. That includes: its background, popular activities, and its staple The Three Monkeys Pub. After reading this page, you will be ready to visit the Eilat!

City Background

Eilat is the city of adventure. If you are an outdoorsy, thrill-seeker – this city is for you. Eilat is located at the southern tip of Israel and remains its passageway to the Red Sea. The city has become a popular tourist resort and port (that rhymes). An interesting fact is that through the port, it actually welcomed Moroccan immigrants through the port.

Wild Activities

For this section, I am going to do a quick overview of the activities you can engross yourself in while visiting Eilat. The most popular activity to do is to snorkel. I am afraid of sharks and am petrified to do this, so I have never done this in Eilat. If you are looking for something budget-friendly, research to find a sunset sail and snorkel tour. Another way to avoid the sharks, is to do underwater observation from Marine Park. This attraction features turtle and stingray pools as well! You can also swim with the dolphins at Dolphin Reef. Diverting from water, you can take a walk through Red Canyon.

The Three Monkeys Pub

Now the restaurant I am picking for Eilat is actually a pub. It is called The Three Monkeys Pub. This spot offers a perfect place for some late night fun. There is great music constantly playing and at night, the stage opens to a gaggle of international performers. Every night you can see someone new and have a blast. If you want to meet likeminded, adventurous people, go to The Three Monkeys Pub to find an adventure buddy.
